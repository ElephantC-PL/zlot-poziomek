**Campus Domasławice**  
**Domasławice 30**  
**Lower Silesian Voivodeship**  
[Mapy.com](https://mapy.com/s/fesovogune)

You can reach the Main Rally Base in the following ways:

1. By bicycle — which we strongly encourage.  
A prize is planned for the participant who arrives by bicycle from the farthest location (regardless of the type of bike).

2. By car — parking will be available for participants.  
Fuel is expensive — consider option 1 and claim the prize for arriving by bike from the farthest location!

3. By train to **Grabowno Wielkie** station near **Twardogóra** (regional and express trains).  
Please note: before traveling, check the train timetable carefully — it tends to change due to maintenance works.  

Consider option 1 and claim the prize for arriving by bike from the farthest location!  

If you still choose to come by train, the distance from the railway station to the Main Rally Base is only 13.5 km — but watch out for local bars, where you may run into rally participants.  

Do not be provoked, stay on your route, and begin your integration only at the Main Rally Base.
